## Intro to the Project

Triggo is an innovative automation platform designed to simplify and optimize your digital life. Inspired by popular services like IFTTT and Zapier, Triggo allows users to create powerful automations by interconnecting various services and applications. Whether you want to automatically save email attachments to a OneDrive folder or send a message on Teams when an issue is created on GitHub, Triggo makes it all possible with an intuitive interface and robust features.

## How to Access Triggo

### Windows (x64)
- **Download**: Visit the [Triggo website](https://triggo.space) and navigate to the downloads section.
- **Install**: Download the installer, run it, and follow the on-screen instructions to install Triggo on your system.

### Android
- **Download**: Open the Google Play Store and search for "Triggo" or [click here](https://play.google.com/.
- **Install**: Tap the "Install" button and wait for the app to be downloaded and installed on your device.

### iOS
- **Download**: Open the App Store and search for "Triggo" or [click here](https://apps.apple.com/).
- **Install**: Tap "Get" to download and install the app on your iOS device.

## Getting Started

### Registering an Account
1. Visit the [Triggo website](https://triggo.space) or open the app on your chosen platform.
2. Click or tap on "Sign Up."
3. Fill in the required details:
   - Email address
   - Password
   - Name (optional)
4. Accept the terms of service and privacy policy.
5. Complete the CAPTCHA (if applicable) and submit the form.
6. Confirm your email address by clicking the link sent to your inbox.

### Logging In
1. Open the app or website.
2. Enter your registered email address and password.
3. Click or tap on "Log In."
4. If two-factor authentication (2FA) is enabled, input the verification code sent to your email or phone.

---

## Creating Your First Automation

1. **Go to the Automation Page**:
   - In the app or web interface, navigate to the "Automations" section.

2. **Start Creating**:
   - Click or tap "Create New Automation."

3. **Select a Trigger**:
   - Choose the event that will initiate the automation (e.g., "New Email Received").

4. **Choose an Action**:
   - Define what should happen when the trigger occurs (e.g., "Save Attachment to OneDrive").

5. **Customize Parameters**:
   - Adjust the available parameters for both the trigger and the action to suit your needs.

6. **Enable the Automation**:
   - Once satisfied, toggle the activation switch to enable the automation.

---

## Linking an Integration

1. **Access the Integration Page**:
   - Navigate to the "Integrations" section of the app or website.

2. **Select a Service**:
   - Browse the list of supported services (e.g., Google, Facebook, GitHub, etc.).

3. **Connect Your Account**:
   - Click on the service you wish to integrate and follow the prompts to authenticate and grant necessary permissions.

4. **Verify Connection**:
   - Ensure the integration is listed as "Connected" in your account settings.
